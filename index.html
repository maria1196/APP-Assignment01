<p>Afghani Mantu is a delicious traditional dish from Afghanistan. It consists of dumplings filled with seasoned ground meat, usually beef or lamb, and onions. The dumplings are then steamed and served with a flavorful tomato-based sauce and topped with yogurt and garnishes like dried mint. It's a popular dish in Afghan cuisine and is often enjoyed as a special treat during celebrations or family gatherings.</p>
